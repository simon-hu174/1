About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and see how long you can last against the relentless horde!

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Attacks are automatic, so focus on positioning and collecting experience orbs to level up. Choose upgrades wisely to create powerful synergies between your abilities!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from various unique characters, each with their own starting weapons and special abilities.

Upgradeable Weapons

Collect and upgrade different weapons and passive items to create your ultimate monster-slaying build.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. How long can you survive?

Tips for Success

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize leveling up your main weapon first. Don't forget to collect treasure chests for special items that can turn the tide of battle!
